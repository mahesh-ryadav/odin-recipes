Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, and creamy cheese.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles
  2. Prepare the meat sauce
  3. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese
  4. Bake until golden
