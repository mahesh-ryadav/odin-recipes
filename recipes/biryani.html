Chicken Biryani

Description

Chicken biryani is a flavorful Indian rice dish cooked with aromatic spices and tender chicken.

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken
  • Yogurt
  • Onions
  • Biryani spices

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken
  2. Cook rice partially
  3. Layer rice and chicken
  4. Cook on low heat
